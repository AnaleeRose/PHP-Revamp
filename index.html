Imagine Dragons

About

Rock band formed in 2008 and relocated in Las Vegas, Nevada.

Dan Reynolds is the only original band member left.

Band Members

Dan Reynolds - Guitars

Daniel Wayne Sermon - Cellos

Daniel Platzman - Drum Kits

Ben McKee - Bass Guitar
